Our Team

Sara Daqiq

Executive Director

Sara Daqiq brings over 15 years of combined teaching and technology experience, including significant contributions to Silicon Valley tech startups. With a proven track record of innovation, she has served in roles such as Software Architect and Senior Software Engineer, leading strategic technical initiatives and reducing operational costs. Currently, she is building a startup focused on leveraging technology to drive impactful solutions. Her expertise spans privacy solutions, API platforms, and curriculum development, further demonstrated through her leadership in organizations like Uber, Okta, and The Harvard Extension School.

Gulafroz Dailey

Operations Officer

Gulafroz Dailey is an accomplished Operations Officer with extensive experience in education, data engineering, and international development. At UNESCO, she played a pivotal role in designing and implementing programs that empowered underserved communities through education and resource allocation. Her tenure at UNESCO highlighted her ability to lead complex operations and ensure impactful outcomes. Combining her expertise in data automation and management with her passion for global outreach, Gulafroz continues to drive efficiency and innovation in every initiative she leads.

Shakiba Daqiq

Chief Technology Officer

Shakiba Daqiq is a dynamic Chief Technology Officer with a strong foundation in technology, education, and global outreach. While studying at Wellesley College and MIT, she taught programming to students in Afghanistan, working closely with field coordinators to distribute funds and organize impactful classes. As the founding engineer of Afghan Girls Build, she established a programming boot camp that expanded access to technical education, empowering students to secure internships and complete impactful projects. She continues to mentor aspiring technologists, fostering innovation and diversity in the global tech industry.
